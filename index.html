About

Welcome to True Grit

True Grit Barbershop was established in 2016.

We are exceptionally well versed in all styles of haircuts and we also offer an expert straight razor shave experience. We provide a clean and fun environment and welcome all to come enjoy a cold beverage and good conversation!

STAY GRITTY!
